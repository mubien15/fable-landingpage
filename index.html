What is Fable

An AI coach for the conversations most people avoid.

Real conversations — feedback, negotiations, confrontations — are rarely taught. We learn them by getting them wrong, which is expensive when careers and relationships are on the line.

Fable gives you a safe space to rehearse. Pick a scenario, choose how hard you want to be pushed — light, medium, or high pressure — and talk it through with an AI that plays the other side authentically. Get honest, specific feedback on what worked and what didn't. Then go again.

30 scenarios. 30 days. That's the programme. And with more than 30 scenarios already built — and more on the way — there's always a conversation worth practising.

How it works

Three steps to a better conversation

01

Pick your scenario

Choose from more than 30 real-world conversations across performance, negotiation, leadership, and more. Each one is built from lived professional experience.

02

Set your pressure level

Choose light, medium, or high pushback. The AI plays the other person — your manager, your client, your examiner — and adapts its resistance to match the challenge you're ready for.

03

Get honest feedback

After each session, Fable breaks down what landed, what fell flat, and what habit to work on next. Then you go again.
